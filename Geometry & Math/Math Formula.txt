▣ Series

	• nc1 + nc2 + nc3 + .... + nCn = (2^n) - 1
	• 1^2 + 2^2 + 3^2 + 4^2 + ...... + n^2 = (n * (n + 1)(2 * n + 1)) / 6
	• 2^0 + 2^1 + 2^3 + ....... + 2^n = (2^(n+1)) - 1

▣ Formula

	• nCr = (nC(r-1)) * ((n-r+1)/r)

▣ Bitwise

	• 2^n = (1 << n)
	• n / 2 = (n >> 1)

▣ Other

	• non-empty subarray = (n*(n+1))/2
	• non-empty subsequence = (2^n) - 1
